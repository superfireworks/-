Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how and when Lenovo Companion - Mobile collects and uses your information. By accessing or using this mobile application, you consent to this Privacy Policy.
Lenovo may modify this Privacy Policy at any time effective upon its posting. Your continued use of this mobile application constitutes your acceptance to this Privacy Policy and any updates.

Information Collection and Use

Lenovo Companion - Mobile may automatically collect information about your device including, but not limited to, your mobile device identifier and hardware information, IP address, device model, the operation system, screen resolution, system languages, network operator, mobile carrier, visited screens and pages, access time, location, search terms, and cookie information.

When you decide to send us feedback about issues for which you cannot find a solution in the app, you should input your service issue and your country before submitting your feedback. At the same time we will automatically collect your device model and device IMEI number to better support your issue. If you do not want us to collect your IMEI number please cancel the feedback collection window when it pops up. The feedback and information you send to us will be sent to Lenovo internal Mobile Support group by secured email. The support team will manage the emails and improve solutions per your feedback. After processing the information will be permanently deleted.

We may combine information you give us with other information from Lenovo resources, transactions and communications. This may include but not limited to information from Lenovo service centers, direct calls, emails and chats, events, information collected by support.lenovo.com and forums.lenovo.com. We may also combine that information with data that is publicly available and data from third parties, such as public information from social media. We may use your information to do the following:

  • Enhance, customize, and personalize your Lenovo experiences and communications
  • Operate, provide, improve and maintain Lenovo’s products and services
  • Analyze user behavior on the application to improve future user experiences
  • Communicate with you about our products, services, and events, and for other promotional purposes
  • We will not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If we become aware that a child under 16 has provided us with personal information, it is our intention to take steps to remove such information.

Lenovo Companion - Mobile uses Google Analytics to collect certain performance-related usage information. You can learn more about how Google Analytics handles and stores this information by reviewing Google Analytics’ privacy policy.

Information Sharing

We may provide your information to Lenovo’s affiliated companies all over the world to improve their services or products.
We may transfer your information to Lenovo contracted service providers all over the world for data management and information analysis.
We may disclose information upon governmental request, in response to a court order, when required by law, or to enforce our policies. Your information may be transferred to a third party as a part of our business assets in a sale of a part or all of Lenovo.

For Lenovo’s complete notice see Lenovo's Privacy Policy. Questions about this statement or about Lenovo's handling of your information may be sent using our short Privacy Submission Form.
